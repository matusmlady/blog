Overbooked on Wizzair flight

  • I read a travel article that stated that if you want to get a better seat at your flight, it is recommended to checkin as late as possible. This is because the good seats - window and aisle, are not automatically assigned to the people checking in with random seat allocation, because the airlines want to sell these good seats for people willing to pay extra up to the very last moments. Armed with this knowledge I did the checkin just one hour before the 3hour to departure deadline for Wizzair. The app let me complete the checkin and said 2 things. Firstly it asked me that in case of overbooking if I want to volunteer for a 100EUR voucher for Wizzair. Secondly it let me complete the checkin process successfuly stating that everything is okay and I should get my boarding pass in person at the checkin. Only once I arrived at the airport the lady doing the process let me know that the flight is overbooked and that I have a chance to get on the plane because 2 passangers didn't check in yet for free online, however they could still come and checkin for extra payment. I received the physical boarding pass with seat assigned with the number 0 meaning no seat assigned for me. Everything proceeded normally. After getting in line at the gate I was asked to board as the very last passenger, because that's when they know if there is any free space left. I was there waiting like this with 2 other gentlemen. After everyone boarded the lady at the gate still waited as she saw some people trying to get through security. They also announced in the airport that any remaining passengers should get to the gate immediately. After multiple announcements I was told I have a seat. The other 2 gentlemen weren't that lucky. I believe this was because there was only 1 seat left as 2 people came through the security at the last second and also I checked in for the physical boarding pass before the guys. So it is important to checkin the soonest possible if you are not assigned a seat. Also don't accept compensation that is generally super low compared to compensation guaranteed by EU for a situation like this, which is monetary compensation + another flight. If time is not presurring you, you can perhaps try to be overbooked on purpose.
