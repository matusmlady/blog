Pea sprouts

  • 10 days ideal for antihistamines.
  • Before starting take out weird looking peas.
  • Every 12 hours do a checkup and clean with water. Take out seed coats that start to peel off. Be careful not to destroy tiny sprouts. Take out deformed and after a couple of days also too small sprouts.
  • Keep covered by blanket in darkness, so they are stronger.
