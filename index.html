Vecerny beh

Povedal som si, ze si pojdem zabehat. Samozrejme som sa k tomu dostal az o 9 vecer, hoci som na to myslel uz omnoho skor. Uz to bolo 4.-krat co som behal 4 kilometre. Behavam na boso, v ten vecer som dal rekordny cas pod 25 minut, 17 koleciek po trave vo vnutornom okraji 250 metrovej drahy. Po dobehani som sa vychodil, zobral som si veci a zamieril som k vychodu. Brana bola zamknuta. Pred nou som si vsimol policajne auto. Kazdy vecer policajti drahu chodia zamykat. Rozmyslal som co spravim. Nakoniec som sa rozhodol preliezt. Prestrcil som cez branu topanky, prehodil cez plot bundu a ze sa teda idem stverat cez plot - hned pred policajtami. V tom prisla zpoza rohu pani policajtka a vravi: "Pockajte. Otvorim Vam." Potom som jej povedal ze tam beha este jedna pani. Spytala sa ma, ci neviem, kolko tam uz je. Odpovedam, ze dlho.

Cordoba / Spain erasmus tips

  • You can fly to both Sevilla and Malaga. Both cities have a bus and train connection with Cordoba. Take a flight arriving before night / leaving later than early morning as no connections from / to cordoba are super early / super late.
  • Carry cash as some places dont accept card below a certain limit.
  • For withdrawal without fees use Cajasur ATMs with Revolut card.
  • Get a room with its own AC, not a central one for the apartment.
  • Checkout Erasmus Student Network and Erasmus Family. They offer WhatsApp groups to connect with other Erasmus students. Each week they organise many free events. They also organize many one day and multiple day trips for affordable prices so you can enjoy Spain and meet new people.
  • Buy an ESN card for a 10% + free 20kg luggage Ryanair discount. You will need a small photo.
  • Both ESN and Erasmus Family cards offer you discounts for their trips.
  • For cheaper busses get a student bus card. You can recharge the card online for free here.
  • For a montly train ticket to campus Rabanales for 30e fill out a form at the trainstation. You will need a small photo.
  • You can also buy a 10 trip ticket for 10e without registration.
  • To buy general things you can use a chinese bazar, as they have a huge sortiment.
  • Many stores like super markets are closed on Sundays and holidays. Smaller lie Proxi or Carrefour however stay opened.
  • In order to access some clubs / buy alcohol you should carry your ID on you at all times.

Revolut tips

  • Revolut has the lowest exchange rates on the market.
  • With the free plan every rolling month you can
    1. exchange up to 1000 euros in your account currency
    2. zero Revolut fees for ATM withdrwal up to 200 euros in your currency
  • ATM's bank may however still charge you as both parties typically have fees.
  • You can check when does your account's rolling month start in the app.
  • Every couple of days/weeks there is a new referral bonus for inviting your friends.
  • The best refferal bonus is the guaranteed 40 euros for the inviting person.
  • You receive these once
    1. your refferal signs up via your link
    2. they order a physical debit card for 7 euros
    3. they make 3 purchases of minimum 5 euros with their account's card
  • You can use one time disposable cards to protect your privacy
  • Exchange rates are 1% more expensive during weekends.

Ryanair tips

  • You can take a free bag with you with the dimensions 40 * 30 * 20 cm.
  • Do not choose a seat on the website as this costs extra. Click on continue with random assignment / remove the chosen seat at checkout.
  • You need to check in online from 24 hours to 2 hours before the flight.
  • Boarding passes are now only digital, however you can still get a physical one before boarding if you checked in online.
  • Self check in luggage for larger bags is now common. You scan your boarding pass and the machine prints you a luggage sticker with code. You put it on the bag yourself or you can ask for help from the airport staff.
  • Onboard you can carry 10 100ml containers of liquids in a transparent bag. Beware sometimes they provide the main plastic bag and 10 containers will not fit in so take less.
  • Dont forget any liquids (creams, disinfection, eye drops) inside your bag, as they may unpack your stuff to check it out.
  • If you have prohibited things like too much liquids or things with sharp edges you will need to throw them out.
  • In order to save weight and volume in your checkin luggage make use of pockets on your jacket and trousers. Do clothes layering.
  • Chew a chewing gum, multiple of them in fact during takeoff and landing if you have problems with the change of pressure.

Slovakia tips

  • Operator O2 offers cheap phone number program called O2 volnost (=freedom). You can call anyone in EU for 15 cents per call/hour.
  • Operator 4ka offers data 1GB for 4 weeks for just 2 euros.

Czech republic tips

  • 1 euro is equal to 25 czech crowns (CZK).
  • You can get a credit number from Telecom for just 200 CZK.
  • Raifansen offers free account for students.
  • For withdrawal without fees use Raifansen ATMs with Revolut card.
  • To get CZK into your Raifansen account for free, withdraw them first with Revolut card. Next insert Raifansen card and put the money back into the ATM.
